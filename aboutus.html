Our Story

How the Ezitech Interview Bot was born

2023

The Hiring Challenge

As the founder of my own software house, I found myself spending countless hours interviewing candidates. The process was time-consuming and often inefficient, with many candidates not being the right fit despite looking good on paper.

Early 2024

The "Aha!" Moment

After yet another marathon interview session, I realized I needed a better way to screen candidates before the live interview. That's when the idea struck me - why not create an AI-powered chatbot that could conduct initial interviews for me?

Mid 2024

Building the First Prototype

I started developing a simple chatbot that could ask technical questions and evaluate responses. The initial version used basic NLP to understand candidate answers and could assess fundamental technical skills.

Late 2024

Adding Emotion AI

Recognizing that technical skills weren't everything, I integrated emotion recognition to analyze candidates' facial expressions and tone during video responses. This helped assess communication skills and cultural fit.

2025

Ezitech Interview Bot is Born

After months of refinement and testing with real candidates, the system proved so effective that I decided to productize it. What began as a personal tool for my software house is now helping other tech leaders streamline their hiring process.
